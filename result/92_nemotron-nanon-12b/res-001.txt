The implication `street_state(wet) ⇒ weather_state(raining)` **cannot be derived** from Γ. The premises only allow conclusions **in the forward direction** (e.g., `raining → wet`, `wet → slippery`), not the reverse. This is a classic case of confusing **implication** with **equivalence**.
